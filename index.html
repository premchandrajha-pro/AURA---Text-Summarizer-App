Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the platform and billing.

How accurate is the summarization? +
Our AI models are trained on billions of parameters to ensure extremely high accuracy and context preservation. It consistently highlights the most crucial information while discarding filler content.
Do you store my text? +
No. We have a strict zero-retention policy. The text you paste is processed entirely in memory and immediately discarded. We never use your data to train our models.
What payment methods do you accept? +
We securely accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, and direct wire transfers for annual Enterprise plans.
Is there a limit on input length? +
Free and Starter plans support up to 5,000 words per request. Our Pro plan extends this to 50,000 words, allowing you to summarize entire books or lengthy reports in one go.
